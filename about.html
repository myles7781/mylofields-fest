About the Festival

Imagine a cross between Glastonbury and Burning Man. You've just imagined Mylofields, a high-minded, spiritually aware, artistic festival, where creativity, compassion and debauchery merge, to create a weekend unrivalled anywhere in the festival calendar.

Our History

Mylofields has a long and proud history, with it's origins being traced back to March 2021, though historians differ on the exact date. What began as the afternoon daydreams of one man, quickly materialised into the greatest show on earth.

Who is Mylo?

The question is not 'Who is Mylo?', the question is, what is Mylo? Mylo is an energy. Mylo is cosmic radiation. Mylo is the sunrise over the Himalayas. Mylo is the feeling you get when you dive gracefully into cool water on a scorching day. Mylo is...you get the idea.
